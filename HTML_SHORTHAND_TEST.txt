*PG The domestic cat (Felis silvestris catus or Felis catus)is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal. They are often
*PG called house cats when kept as indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and
*PG felines. They are often valued by humans for companionship and for their ability to hunt vermin. There are more than 
*PG seventy cat breeds recognized by various cat registries.

*H2 Cats as Hunters
*BR
*PG Cats are similar in anatomy to the other felids, with a strong flexible body, quick reflexes, sharp retractable claws and
*PG teeth adapted to killing small prey. Cat senses fit a crepuscular and predatory ecological niche. Cats can hear sounds too
*PG faint or too high in frequency for human ears, such as those made by mice and other small animals. They can see in 
*EM near darkness.
